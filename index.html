Things Skylandia Does That You Haven't Seen Before

ElytraSwap repairs your elytras in mid-air without landing. It throws Bottles o' Enchanting upward at a configured angle so the XP orbs arc back down onto you. When the bottles run out, it opens shulker boxes in your inventory to get more. Your elytra supply manages itself for the entire session.
GrimEfly detects portal traps before you fly into them. On every chunk load, it scans incoming terrain for portal frame structures. If one is within threshold distance of your highway, Baritone reroutes around it before you're ever in range.
ChunkChestGrid handles its own supply chain. Runs out of chests → opens your shulker boxes. Those are empty too → navigates to trees → chops them → crafts more chests → continues the 50×50 chunk grid. Seven states. No input required.
DemonCrystal is a self-tuning combat AI with 50 setting groups. It tracks damage-per-second, hit success rate, and strategy effectiveness per fight and adjusts placement range, rotation speed, burst timing, and crystal count automatically. It runs eight attack strategies and switches between them via a live fallback system when the current one stops working.
Journey Recorder's AI knows who you are. Provide a user profile ("solo stash hunter, values secrecy") and a story theme ("noir"). The AI writes your sessions as your character, in your genre. Incremental refinement rewrites the story as you play so the final output is a cohesive narrative.
Multiple Skylandia instances share their exploration data in real time. CoordPoppy uses Meteor's Swarm system to sync chunk classifications, entity positions, and Trails data across all connected accounts every 10 seconds.
TrailFollower has a POI block list. Add any block type. When those blocks appear in a newly loaded chunk, TrailFollower steers toward it. Add crying obsidian. Add ancient debris. It hunts whatever you configure.
Trails fires Discord @mentions for specific chunk types. Configure a separate Discord webhook and ping ID per type. Flags only TICK_EXPLOIT chunks — the freshest player signatures on the map — and pings you directly.

How Modules Talk to Each Other

This is not a collection of isolated features. Systems are designed to feed each other.

Trails classifies every loaded chunk (8 methods, 5 types)
    ↓ feeds bearing weights to
TrailFollower steers toward old/player-touched terrain
    with POI block steering, Destination waypoint mode,
    per-waypoint Discord webhooks, anti-AFK randomization
    ↓ hands long legs to
AFKVanillaFly sustains flight through a path
    that routes specifically through those old chunks
    ↓ all three feed data to
CoordPoppy maps the operation, overlays Trails types,
    shows TrailFollower's bearing, runs Randar on players,
    syncs everything to swarm-connected accounts every 10s
    fires Discord webhooks on entity/structure detection
