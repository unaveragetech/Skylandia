What Skylandia Does

Not a module pack. A toolkit built by people who play anarchy seriously.

🏭 Autonomous Exploration

Trail-following systems that scan, classify, and navigate toward player-touched terrain across thousands of blocks — while you're AFK.

💎 Crystal PvP Engine

60-tick movement prediction. Five multi-crystal modes. Pattern-based placement geometry. The full crystal loop, automated.

📚 AI Story Generation

Journey Recorder tracks what you do and turns it into readable narrative using HuggingFace AI. Your sessions become stories.

📦 Dimension Logistics

Two-account automated shulker transfer between Overworld and End using stasis chamber coordination and encrypted inter-account chat.

👀 ESP & Render Suite

PotESP, MobGearESP, DroppedItemESP, EntityClusterESP, HoleESP, CrystalPositionESP, DamageNumbers, and more — all configurable.

📄 Minescript Integration

Write, edit, save, and run Minescript Python scripts directly inside Minecraft. Extend Skylandia with your own automation logic.

Access

Skylandia is closed. Every copy is individually authorized. The mod verifies your identity on every launch before anything loads. How it works is not documented here — that's intentional.

🔒

Member-Only

Authorized users only. No public download. No trial. Your account must be registered.

✅

Verified Each Launch

Identity is checked on every session start. Nothing loads until verification passes.

👤

Earned, Not Bought

Access comes through Lotus Clan membership or genuine contribution. Not through payment.

How to Get In

  1. 1. Join the Discord and introduce yourself.
  2. 2. Contribute to the clan, the codebase, or the community.
  3. 3. Wait for leadership review. Approved members get a registered build.
