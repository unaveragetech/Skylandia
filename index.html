Access & Security

Skylandia is a closed, member-only addon. Every copy is individually authorized — the mod verifies your identity on every launch before any features become available.

🔒 Whitelist Only

Access is granted exclusively to approved Lotus Clan members and contributors. Your username must appear in the authorized list embedded inside the JAR itself — no external list, no workaround.

👤 UUID Verification

Beyond a username check, Skylandia optionally verifies your Mojang UUID against a registered mapping. Even if someone knows your username, they cannot load the mod without your exact account UUID matching the record.

📄 Per-Launch Validation

The whitelist is regenerated and cryptographically re-signed from the JAR on every single launch. Local copies are never trusted — the JAR is always the source of truth, making it impossible to tamper with access records between sessions.

🔐 Signature Verification

Every whitelist file is cryptographically signed at write time. If the signature is missing, expired, or does not match — access is denied to everyone, even valid users. There is no silent fallback or grace mode.

🏭 API-Backed Identity

UUID lookups are performed against the official Mojang API with an automatic fallback to a secondary source for reliability. Your identity is confirmed against Mojang's own records, not just a local string.

Want Access?

Access is earned, not purchased. Join the Lotus Clan Discord, contribute to the community, and get reviewed. Approved members are added to the whitelist and receive an authorized build.
