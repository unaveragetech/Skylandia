🛠 Feature Overview

🧭 Exploration & Hunting

  • ActivatedSpawnerDetector — Pinpoints active mob spawners
  • CaveDisturbanceDetector — Detects signs of player presence
  • BaseFinder — Identifies base‑like structures
  • StashBotModule — Auto‑logs stashes with waypoints
  • BetterStashFinder — Enhanced stash detection
  • SearchBot — Automated pattern searching
  • TerrainAnalyzer — Strategic terrain profiling

👁️ Render & ESP

  • HoleAndTunnelESP — Navigation terrain features
  • MobGearESP — Finds mobs with player loot
  • DroppedItemESP — Tracks valuable drops
  • EntityClusterESP — Shows mob/player clusters
  • CrystalPositionESP — PvP crystal placements
  • DamageNumbersModule — Real‑time damage readouts

🤖 Automation

  • ElytraSwap — Auto chestplate/elytra toggle
  • AutoEnchant — Automated enchanting
  • BaritonePathing — Full navigation integration
  • DemonCrystal — Crystal PvP automation
  • ShulkerTransportModule — Inventory logistics
  • Printer — Automated building

🛡️ Utility

  • GrimEfly — Advanced Elytra flight
  • SecureChatModule — Encrypted messaging
  • SignHistorian — Archives sign text history
  • Pitch40Util — Optimal pitch locking
  • OllamaBotModule — AI‑powered assistant
  • AutoLoginModule — Streamlined login
